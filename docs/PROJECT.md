# ScandiTech Digital Installation Standard (DIS)
## Fire Protection Edition

## 1. Product Identity

Digital Installation Standard for Fire Protection.

A structured, deterministic compliance intelligence platform that converts
manufacturer-tested fire protection systems into standardized digital installation logic.

ScandiTech is the sole publisher and content authority.
Manufacturers are data sources, not platform operators.

---

## 2. Mission

To standardize how fire protection systems are interpreted, selected,
and executed in construction projects.

The platform provides:
- Structured evaluation
- Multi-manufacturer compliant solutions
- Clear installation requirements
- Standard Solution Reference (SSR)
- Exportable Solution Pack

The platform does NOT certify installation execution.

---

## 3. Product Scope

The system supports all fire protection domains:

### A) Penetrations
- Single penetrations (cable, pipe, conduit)
- Mixed penetrations
- Cable trays and bundles
- Ventilation ducts (round and rectangular)
- Insulated services
- Wall and floor penetrations

### B) Linear Joint Seals
- Wall-to-floor joints
- Wall-to-wall joints
- Movement joints

### C) Board Encasements
- Duct encasements
- Structural protection
- Service boxing

### D) Fire Dampers
- Placement rules
- Clearance requirements
- Access rules

All domains supported structurally from day one.

---

## 4. User Types

### Internal (ScandiTech)
- super_admin
- content_admin
- reviewer

### External (Customers)
- customer_admin
- field_user

Manufacturers do not have login access.

---

## 5. Core Output

- List of compliant solutions
- Structured installation requirements
- Materials list
- Steps
- References
- Standard Solution Reference (SSR)
- Exportable Solution Pack (PDF)

---

## 6. Architectural Principles

- API-first
- Server-side compliance logic
- Deterministic rule evaluation
- Version-controlled solutions
- Publisher-controlled content
- Extensible multi-discipline structure
- No installation certification (Phase 1)
